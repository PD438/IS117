Sweet Potato Casserole

This sweet potato casserole is a delicious and comforting dish perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup melted butter (for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mashed sweet potatoes, granulated sugar, beaten eggs, 1/2 cup melted butter, milk, vanilla extract, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Mix until well combined.
  3. Transfer the sweet potato mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish and spread it evenly.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the chopped pecans, brown sugar, flour, and 1/3 cup melted butter to make the topping.
  5. Sprinkle the pecan topping evenly over the sweet potato mixture.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the casserole is heated through.
  7. Allow the casserole to cool slightly before serving.
  8. Enjoy your sweet potato casserole!
