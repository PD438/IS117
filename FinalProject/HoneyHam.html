Delicious Honey Glazed Ham Recipe

This honey glazed ham is perfect for any special occasion. The sweet and savory glaze will have everyone coming back for seconds!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (8-10 pound) fully cooked, bone-in ham
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325째F (165째C).
  2. Place the ham in a roasting pan and score the surface in a diamond pattern.
  3. In a small saucepan, combine the honey, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, cloves, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the sugar is dissolved and the glaze is smooth.
  4. Brush half of the glaze over the ham, making sure to get it into the scored cuts.
  5. Cover the ham loosely with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 1 1/2 to 2 hours, or until heated through.
  6. Remove the foil and brush the remaining glaze over the ham. Increase the oven temperature to 400째F (200째C) and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the glaze is caramelized and the ham is golden brown.
  7. Allow the ham to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
  8. Enjoy your delicious honey glazed ham!
