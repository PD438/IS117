Amazing Steak Fries Recipe

These steak fries are crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. Perfect as a side dish or a snack!

Ingredients:

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Wash and cut the potatoes into thick wedges.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the potato wedges with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, black pepper, thyme, and rosemary until evenly coated.
  4. Arrange the potato wedges in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the fries are golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.
  7. Enjoy your amazing steak fries!
