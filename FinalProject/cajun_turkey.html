Perfect Cajun Turkey Recipe

This Cajun turkey recipe is perfect for any occasion. Enjoy the flavorful and juicy turkey with your family and friends!

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole turkey (12-14 pounds), thawed
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for extra heat)
  • 1 cup chicken broth

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325째F (165째C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, Cajun seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, thyme, oregano, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper.
  3. Pat the turkey dry with paper towels. Rub the seasoning mixture all over the turkey, including under the skin and inside the cavity.
  4. Place the turkey on a rack in a roasting pan. Pour the chicken broth into the bottom of the pan.
  5. Roast the turkey in the preheated oven for about 3 to 3.5 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C) in the thickest part of the thigh.
  6. Baste the turkey with the pan juices every 30 minutes to keep it moist.
  7. Once done, remove the turkey from the oven and let it rest for 20-30 minutes before carving.
  8. Enjoy your perfect Cajun turkey!
