Lemon Pasta Pronto Recipe

This quick and easy lemon pasta recipe is perfect for a light and refreshing meal. Enjoy the zesty lemon flavor combined with garlic and Parmesan cheese!

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup pasta cooking water

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/4 cup of the pasta cooking water and then drain the pasta.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the lemon zest and juice to the skillet and stir to combine.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat in the lemon and garlic mixture.
  5. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and toss to combine. If the pasta seems dry, add the reserved pasta cooking water a little at a time until the desired consistency is reached.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and stir in the chopped parsley.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
