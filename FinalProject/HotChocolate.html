Delicious Hot Chocolate Recipe

This rich and creamy hot chocolate is perfect for warming up on a cold day. Enjoy with marshmallows or whipped cream!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it is hot but not boiling.
  2. Whisk in the cocoa powder, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined and smooth.
  3. Continue to heat the mixture, stirring occasionally, until it is hot and steaming.
  4. Pour the hot chocolate into mugs and top with whipped cream or marshmallows if desired.
  5. Enjoy your homemade hot chocolate!
