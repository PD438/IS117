Delicious Beef Ribs Recipe

This mouth-watering beef ribs recipe is perfect for any occasion. Enjoy tender and flavorful ribs with your favorite sides!

Ingredients:

  • 4 pounds beef ribs
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup paprika
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 275째F (135째C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, paprika, black pepper, salt, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper.
  3. Rub the spice mixture all over the beef ribs, ensuring they are well coated.
  4. Place the ribs on a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil.
  5. Cover the ribs with another sheet of aluminum foil and seal the edges to create a packet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 3 hours, or until the ribs are tender.
  7. Remove the top layer of foil and brush the ribs with barbecue sauce.
  8. Increase the oven temperature to 350째F (175째C) and bake for an additional 30 minutes, or until the sauce is caramelized.
  9. Allow the ribs to rest for a few minutes before serving.
  10. Enjoy your delicious beef ribs!
