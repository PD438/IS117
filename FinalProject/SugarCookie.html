Classic Sugar Cookie Recipe

These classic sugar cookies are perfect for any occasion. Decorate them with your favorite icing and sprinkles!

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 3 tablespoons heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, stir together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth. Beat in the egg, vanilla extract, and almond extract. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients. Add the heavy cream and mix until dough forms.
  4. Roll rounded teaspoonfuls of dough into balls, and place onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  5. Flatten each ball slightly with the bottom of a glass.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes, or until golden. Let cool on wire racks.
  7. Decorate with your favorite icing and sprinkles once cooled.
  8. Enjoy your homemade sugar cookies!
